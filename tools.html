Essential Tools and Their Uses

Plow

The plow is used to break up and turn over soil, preparing it for planting. It helps to mix organic matter into the soil and improve its structure.

Hoe

The hoe is essential for weeding and cultivating soil. It allows farmers to manage weeds and aerate the soil effectively.

Price: $25

Tractor

The tractor is a versatile machine used for a variety of farming tasks, including plowing, planting, and harvesting. It is a crucial tool for modern agriculture.

Seed Drill

The seed drill ensures even planting of seeds and helps to increase crop yield by optimizing seed placement.
